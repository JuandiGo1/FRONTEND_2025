MY MOST PLAYED PS4 GAMES

GTA V

Grand Theft Auto V is an open-world action-adventure game where you can explore the fictional city of Los Santos. You play as three different characters, each with their own unique storylines and missions. The game offers a vast array of activities, from driving and shooting to exploring and completing side quests.

Assasin's Creed Odissey

Set in ancient Greece, Assassin's Creed Odyssey is an action role-playing game where you take on the role of a mercenary during the Peloponnesian War. You can choose to play as either Alexios or Kassandra and embark on a journey to uncover your past while exploring a beautiful open world filled with historical landmarks, quests, and battles.

PES 2021

Pro Evolution Soccer 2021, also known as eFootball PES 2021, is a soccer simulation game that offers a realistic football experience. The game includes various licensed teams, leagues, and players, along with improved gameplay mechanics and graphics. You can compete in different game modes, such as MyClub, Master League, and online matches.

UFC 4

UFC 4 is a mixed martial arts fighting game that brings the excitement of the UFC to your console. You can create your own fighter or play as one of the real-life UFC athletes. The game features various fighting styles, customizable moves, and an in-depth career mode where you can rise through the ranks to become a UFC champion.
